Share your smile with the world

Our mission at the Durban North Smile Center is to help you achieve
your best smile, inside and out.

With this at heart, we have assembled a team of specialists involved in Orthodontics, Speech Therapy, Audiology, Dental Hygiene, Psychology and Periodontics.

Our specialist practitioners provide the best evaluation, planning and treatment that is effected in a positive and supportive environment.

We have many stories of changed lives and positive outcomes, from a cleft lip success story, to a hearing correction that changed the life of a child.
