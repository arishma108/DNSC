Practioners

ORTHODONTIST

Dr Sikki (Surandar) Singh

Graduated as a Dentist at the University of the Witwatersrand (Wits University) in Johannesburg in 1975. He held a junior lecturer position in the department of oral pathology at Wits University during 1975 and 1976. He was Lecturer/Registrar in the Department of Orthodontics at the University of the Western Cape from 1981 to 1986 where He completed an M.Ch.D degree in Orthodontics and graduated as an Orthodontist.
In 1987 Dr Singh relocated from Cape Town to his home town, Durban, and proceeded to open his first private Orthodontic Practice and has been in private practice ever since! His work ethic is governed by excellence and consistency and he has treated several patients over three generations, from the grandparent to the child to the grandchild!
Dr. Singh founded the Wentworth Foundation for Orthodontic and Craniofacial Services rendering free tertiary level dental care in Kwa Zulu Natal (KZN) for the poor who have no access to the treatments available. He has also founded the Academy of Orthodontic and Advanced Dental Assisting to advance the knowledge of Oral Hygienists and Dental Assistants in the field of Orthodontics and Practice Management.

ORAL HYGIENIST

Saras Naidoo

“It is in giving that you receive. Also if you treat people with humility the response you will get will be amazing!”

Saras Naidoo qualified from the University of Kwa-Zulu Natal with a Batchelor of Oral Health Degree. She has been working with Dr. Sikki Singh for 30 years and during this time she has gained a tremendous amount of experience in Orthodontics, Cleft lip & Palate and has further qualified to practice Oral Hygiene as an independent practitioner.

Having been part of the Wentworth foundation for more than 20 years, she says,”working with the underprivileged has taught me humility and gratitude.” Described as approachable and humble she gives her best to every patient, gaining their trust in the process.
When she’s not improving the lives of her patients, Saras loves scouting second hand shops for old or antique furniture for her to restore, giving her a great sense of achievement once completed.

ORAL HYGIENIST

Kathy Dolloway

“She who walks with integrity, walks securely”. (Proverbs 10:9)
Kathy Dolloway qualified as a registered Dental Hygienist at Glasgow Dental Hospital in 1985 after which she practiced Oral Hygiene in three large Dental Practices until moving to Durban in March 2000. Kathy furthered her studies at the University of Durban – Westville in 2001 to include expanded functions in Orthodontics, Radiography, local Anesthetics, Emergency and traumatic restorative techniques. She opened her own practice in June 2015. Further courses in Expanded Function were completed in 2018 at the University of the Western Cape and she has been the Oral Hygienist Association KZN Chairperson since January 2018.
She believes that Oral health is essential to your overall wellness, not just acute health issues but also making a big difference to your image and confidence. Kathy’s approach to patient care is to assess your oral health type and needs, thereafter developing a programme of preventive care including both self-health habits and regular preventative treatments. An ‘afterhours’ landscape designer and avid reader, Kathy’s overall wellness approach resonates with all her patients.

PERIODONTIST

Dr Sankeshan Padayachee

Dr Sankeshan Padayachee is a Periodontist and Oral Medicine Specialist qualified with a BDS from Wits University and a MChD and OMP from the University of the Western Cape. He is the head of Clinical Unit (Oral Medicine and Periodontology) and senior lecturer at the University of the Witwatersrand Dr Padayachee has worked in Port Shepstone and parts of rural Kwa-Zulu Natal during his community service, after which he worked in Cape Town as a dentist and lecturer at the University of the Western Cape. He pursued a career path in specialising in Oral Medicine and Periodontology at the University of the Western Cape and returned to Durban opening a private practice in Umhlanga. He has continued with teaching and training of students and specialists at the University of the Witwatersrand in the Department of Oral Medicine and Periodontology Sankeshan is an executive member of the South African Society for Periodontology, Implantology and Oral Medicine (SASPIO), the International team for Implantology (ITI), South African Dental Association (SADA) and a member and trainer of the Swiss Dental Academy (SDA). His professional attitude is focussed on patient’s happiness and comfort with a service that is based on achieving the highest standard of treatment. Sankeshan works with a hands-on approach and is dedicated to his patient’s and to the profession

CLINICAL PSYCHOLOGIST

Nazia Osman

“Healing isn’t about changing who you are; it’s about changing your relationship to who are you. A fundamental part of that is honouring how you feel.”
Nazia Osman qualified with a Master’s in Social Science Clinical Psychology (cum laude) from the University of KwaZulu Natal. An independent, board-certified Clinical Psychologist registered with the Health Professions Council of South Africa (HPCSA) and a professional service provider registered with the Board of Health Care Funders (BHF). Nazia has extensive work experience as a life coach, hypnosis practitioner and has worked at various hospitals and institutes within KZN namely; UNISA, UKZN, King DiniZulu Hospital Complex, R.K.Khan hospital, McCords Hospital, Kenilworth Respite Centre for victims of Domestic Violence, Mahatma Gandhi Memorial Hospital, Action in Autism and Phoenix Assessment and Therapy Centre. She treats all psychiatric and psychological disorders as well as help with any personal, professional or societal challenges with the best possible professional care that ensures clients feel safe and comfortable, sharing their inner world with her, so that they may journey towards a path of self-discovery and personal enrichment. When you come in for a session with Nazia, you will get a safe, non-judgmental space in which you are free to speak with honesty about what is on your mind, and work together on goals and ways of thinking to change your life and get you closer to where you want to be. An avid reader, researcher, blogger, mental health activist and self-care specialist, Nazia loves helping lift the spirits those less fortunate .
